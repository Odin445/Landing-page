Some inspirational people

  • Albert Einstein was a renowned theoretical physicist best known for developing the theory of relativity, which revolutionized our understanding of space, time, and energy.

  • Mahatma Gandhi was a pivotal leader in the Indian independence movement, advocating for nonviolent resistance and civil disobedience as means to achieve political and social change.

  • Stephen Hawking was a renowned theoretical physicist and cosmologist, best known for his work on black holes and his development of the theory of Hawking radiation.

  • Martin Luther King Jr. was a prominent civil rights leader who championed racial equality and justice through nonviolent protest, most famously leading the movement for the end of racial segregation in the United States.
